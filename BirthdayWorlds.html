Story

How it began?
It all began as my birthday approached this year, I started to wonder what birthday means to everyone.
I wanted to collect people ’ s thoughts about their birthdays.
And then I discovered it was all about memories and imaginations. And I wondered, again, what if I could reconstruct these memories and imaginations abstractly, then feel them by myself?

Then what happened?
I started a mini project to make this idea come to life. With the help from my friend, Loooooner.
I asked my friends to share their memories and imaginations, I also placed a collection box in my community to request help from my neighbors.
To my surprise, a lovely lady generously decorated my box with her own painting brushes. It really warmed my heart.

How it worked?
A lot of AI magic happened here.
I put all the descriptions into prompts, and used Blockade Labs’s Skybox AI to generate skybox images, then turn them into navigable scenes in a website.
I used MusicLM from AI Test Kitchen to generate the music, based on the descriptions I gathered.
Then I connected all the descriptions, skyboxes, and the music, to become a full intractable experience.
I focused mainly on the website structure, UI, and AI generation. Loooooner was the magician setting up Three.js for all the 3D scenes and 3D text.

What else?
This was a hacky project completed in a short time., there was a lot more to polish and to add. But I still want to show it to you during my birthday month.
